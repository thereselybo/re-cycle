Tours

Nøklevann

For the outdoorsy family we can offer this tour revolving Nøklevann east in Oslo. We will stop half-way for a sip of Solo and a Kvikk Lunsj before moving on, enjoying the scenery for the rest of the trip.

Length: 13km

Difficulty: Medium

Time: 1,5 hour

Sightseeing

The people's favorite. This tour takes you around Oslo by the must-sees for a different kind of sightseeing. Spot the Castle, the Opera House and the Angry Boy in the Vigeland Installation - all the while burning off the calories from your brunost breakfast.

Length: 17km

Difficulty: Easy

Time: 1 hour

Holmenkollen

This tour is for the ones who have had a little bit too much coffee, or simply need to burn off some steam. We will guide you up the steep hills towards the splendid views of Holmenkollen, perhaps stopping at Frognerseteren for a piece of the famous apple cake?

Length: 10km

Difficulty: Medium-hard

Time: 1 hour
